Core Values

Chiang Kai Shek College encourages its teachers, staff, and students to lead their lives according to four Confucian values:

禮 Lǐ (Propriety): Good manners, proper behavior; respect for others, especially for elders.

義 Yì (Righteousness): The sense of what is morally right and fair for all.

廉 Lián (Incorruptibility): Avoiding bad influences and not accepting bribes in exchange for favors.

恥 Chǐ (Honor): A sense of shame upon failure to meet reasonable expectations; a conscious effort to always do what is right.

These four values sustain the development of morality (德 dé), wisdom (智 zhì), physical well-being (體 tǐ), social skills (群 qún), and aesthetic appreciation (美 měi).

Based on these Confucian values, CKS College simplifies its Core Values as:

C – Character

K – Knowledge

S – Service

Mission Statement

Chiang Kai Shek College is committed to providing quality education that is grounded on academic excellence, holistic development, and ethical principles. We strive to:

  • Cultivate a dynamic learning environment that fosters critical thinking, creativity, and a passion for lifelong learning
  • Empower students to thrive in a rapidly changing global landscape by helping them excel academically, develop leadership skills, and embrace cultural diversity
  • Promote and preserve the Filipino-Chinese culture by instilling the values of propriety, righteousness, incorruptibility, and honor in our students
  • Create a supportive and inclusive community that promotes personal development, social responsibility, and ethical conduct
  • Engage in research, innovation, and collaboration to enhance educational practices and contribute to national and global development

Vision Statement

Chiang Kai Shek College seeks to be a premier educational institution, recognized locally and globally, that nurtures students to become empowered leaders, lifelong learners, and responsible contributors to society, upholding Filipino-Chinese values, and fostering a sense of unity, excellence, and social responsibility.
